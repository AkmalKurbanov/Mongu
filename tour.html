Itinerary

Arrival in Bishkek and meeting at Manas Airport.

After a short rest and luggage check – transfer to the Karkyra camp, located at an altitude of 2200 meters in a picturesque valley on the eastern border of Kyrgyzstan. Along the way, you will enjoy the first views of majestic mountains, clean mountain air, and picturesque landscapes.

Upon arrival – accommodation in comfortable yurts, which have all the amenities to relax after the trip.

In the evening, you can enjoy the atmosphere of the winter camp.

During these days, you will enjoy ski touring in the areas of Chaarkuduk tract, Tyup, Tuz, and Uch-Kashka gorges. Slopes with an elevation difference of 300–800 meters allow you to combine active skiing with enjoying scenic mountain landscapes and pristine, untouched nature.

At the Karkyra camp, you will find cozy accommodation in heated yurts and full comfort: three meals a day in a buffet format, as well as a warm atmosphere of communication with other tour participants. Every evening is an opportunity to restore energy, share impressions, and simply enjoy the magic of the mountains.

After breakfast – transfer to Bishkek. Upon arrival in the capital, a dinner in a pleasant atmosphere awaits you, where you can summarize the trip and share impressions. After dinner – transfer to Manas Airport for your flight home.

We set up stationary tent camps along the entire route. Every year we improve the infrastructure and increase comfort for tour participants.

If you book this route, you get access to all the amenities of our camps throughout the tour.

What this gives you:

  • Ease and comfort on the way
    You do not carry food and bivouac equipment – everything is already organized

  • Savings on escort
    No need to hire porters and a cook – less expenses, more freedom

  • Safety
    There is communication in every camp, and you also get a detailed map of the route

What is included in the equipment of each stationary tent camp

  • RED FOX tents – capacity 4 people, but designed for accommodation of 2 people in each.
  • TEAM FOX dining tent – capacity 10–20 people.
  • Tents provide 5-centimeter mattresses and sleeping pads for a comfortable sleep.
  • Electricity (from a generator) is available from 19:00 to 22:00 – for charging phones, tablets, and other devices.
  • There is a toilet tent and a shower tent. For the shower, a small basin, a ladle, and warm water are provided (please note that in bad weather, providing warm water can be difficult).
  • Free tea, coffee, sweets, and cookies.
  • Bar with alcohol and drinks (vodka, wine, cognac, juices, etc.) – for an additional fee.
  • Kitchen utensils, tables, and chairs for the convenience of guests.
